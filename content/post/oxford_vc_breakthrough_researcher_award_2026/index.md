I was shortlisted for the **Oxford University Vice-Chancellor's Breakthrough Researcher Award**, recognizing researchers at the early stages of their careers who have made a significant impact at the University.

Our research direction focuses on [Breakthrough Research in Agentic AI Safety and Security](https://hr.admin.ox.ac.uk/vice-chancellors-awards), developing safeguards to identify vulnerabilities in AI agents and prevent them from leaking sensitive information or assisting malicious actors.
